## üìä Next Steps: Submit to Google Search Console

### Step 1: Verify Your Site
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your URL: `https://heicconverter.online`
4. Choose verification method: **HTML tag** (easiest)
5. Copy the meta tag and add it to `/app/frontend/public/index.html` in the `<head>` section
6. Click "Verify"

### Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap
1. Once verified, go to **Sitemaps** in the left menu
2. Enter: `api/sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
4. Google will start crawling your site within 24-48 hours

### Step 3: Request Indexing (Optional but Recommended)
1. Go to **URL Inspection** in the left menu
2. Enter your homepage URL
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages:
   - `/heic-to-jpg`
   - `/heic-to-png`
   - `/heic-to-pdf`
   - `/blog`
